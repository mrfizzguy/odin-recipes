Hamburger Recipe

Description

I enjoy eating burgers

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Beef patty
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Pour cooking oil on a heated pan
  2. Sear beef patty till golden brown on both sides
  3. place lettuce, followed by cheese, then tomato sauce and lastly the beef patty between the burger buns
