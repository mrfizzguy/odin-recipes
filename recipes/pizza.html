Pizza Recipe

Description

I love pineapples on pizza!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pinapples
  • Ham slices
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Put in the following order, tomato sauce, cheese, ham, and pinapples onto the pizza dough
  2. Preheat the oven to 180degres celcius and cook the pizza in the oven for 15 minutes
  3. Sprinkle some garnish and chilli flakes onto the pizza
